About Me

I am a senior at Miramonte High School (MHS). I'm a student journalist, and my interests include math and learning languages. Below are links to some of my projects.

I am also passionate about immigrant and refugee rights. I’m the founder and president of Advocacy for Immigrants and Refugees (AIR MHS) (an organization that supports local immigrant and refugee communities by raising funds and increasing community awareness), the Vice President of Keeping our Promise MHS (which provides English language tutoring to Afghan refugee students), the President of the Middle Eastern Student Association, and the Refugee & Immigrant Acclimation Program Director of the Hafez Foundation.

Some of my hobbies are: reading, hiking, cooking, baking, and sleeping. I’m also a black belt in taekwondo, and I enjoy weightlifting.
