About Me

I am a junior at Miramonte High School. I love learning languages and math. Below you can find links to some of my projects.

I am passionate about immigrant and refugee rights. I’m the founder and president of Advocacy for Immigrants and Refugees at Miramonte (an organization that supports local immigrant and refugee communities by raising funds and increasing community awareness), the Tutoring Coordinator for Keeping our Promise MHS (which provides English language tutoring to Afghan refugee students), the Treasurer of the Middle Eastern Student Association, and the Refugee & Immigrant Acclimation Program Director for the Hafez Foundation.

My hobbies include reading, baking, and eating. I’m also a black belt in taekwondo, and I enjoy weightlifting.
